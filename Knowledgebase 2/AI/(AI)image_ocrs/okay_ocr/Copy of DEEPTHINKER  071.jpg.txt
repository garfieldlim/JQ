
E. Term and Termination

1. This agreement shall be effective upon the signing of this memorandum by both parties and shall remain in force for a period of six (6) months, unless sooner terminated by either party: Provided, however that neither party shall have the right to terminate this agreement during the first three months from its execution.

2. In case of early termination, DEEPTHINKER SOFTWARE DEV. CO., shall notify USJ-R in writing and such notice shall be deemed effective upon receipt by USJ-R: Provided, however, that any termination shall not affect the obligations accrued
