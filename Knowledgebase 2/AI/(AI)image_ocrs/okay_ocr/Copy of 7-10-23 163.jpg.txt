5. Provide a training certificate for each student-trainee who successfully completes the program;
and,

6. Provide USJ-R with periodic reports as to the progress of the students in their respective
assignments and overall performance.

B. USJ-R shall:
1. Endorse to DNI qualified students for on-the-job training under the CORPORATE INTERSHIP PROGRAM;

3. Designate an Industry Coordinator who will be assigned the following responsibilities:

d. Arrange a conference with DNI when student-trainee problem arises;

4. Adhere to all regulations regarding laws and rules and regulation pertaining to internship or

C. The parties shall ensure that the student-trainees:

3. Will be treated as trainees and not as regular employees;
