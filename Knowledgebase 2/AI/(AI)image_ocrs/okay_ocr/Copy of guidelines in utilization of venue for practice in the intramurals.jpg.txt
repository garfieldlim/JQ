August 1, 2019

RE: GUIDELINES IN THE UTILIZATION OF THE VENUE FOR PRACTICES IN THE INTRAMURALS

The coaches of all colleges/departments are directed to comply with the following guidelines in the utilization of the venue for practices in the intramurals:

1. You must secure an Entry Permit from the SSD office and attach the following documents:
a. Photocopy of the approved Activity Permit from VP-SW
b. List of players of every event
c. Schedule and venue of your practices in the Basak Campus

The coach must present the approved Entry Permit and all other attachments upon entry to the campus.

2. No unauthorized personnel, except officials, should be inside the playing court during the entire duration of the practice.
3. Strictly follow your schedule and in case you cancel the use of the venue, inform the Athletics office right away or at least 3 days ahead to give way to other users.
4. Inform your players to observe proper attire upon entering the campus (no shorts, sleeveless, slippers).
5. Remind your players to always observe orderliness and cleanliness in the area of the venue.

These guidelines are for strict implementation.
