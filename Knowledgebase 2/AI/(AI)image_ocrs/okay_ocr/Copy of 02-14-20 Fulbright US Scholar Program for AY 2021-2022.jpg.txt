
Fulbright U.S. Scholar Program for AY 2021-2022 is now accepting applications. The Fulbright U.S. Scholar Program offers approximately 470 teaching, research or combination teaching/research awards in over 125 countries. Opportunities are available for college and university faculty and administrators as well as for professionals, artists, journalists, scientists, lawyers, independent scholars and many others. In addition to several new program models designed to meet the changing needs of U.S. academics and professionals, Fulbright offers flexible awards including multi-country opportunities.

The deadline for applications is September 15, 2020. For application and registration details, please go to: https://www.cies.org/program/fulbright-us-scholar-program.
