Original Letter:

The University of San Jose-Recoletos, located on the corners of P. Lopez and Magallanes Streets in Cebu City, Philippines, offers a degree program in Information, Communication and Technology (ICT).

An invitation letter has been sent to Rev. Fr. Hernando D. Coja, Administrator of the Safety and Security Department at the University of San Jose-Recoletos. The letter is requesting approval for student participants and guests to enter the Basak Campus during a seminar-workshop on Mobile Game Development using Unity, which will be held on February 8 and 15, 2020.

The College of Information, Computer and Communications Technology (CICCT) and Cebu Game Developers (CGD) are hosting the free workshop at BCL 5 of the St. Ezekiel Moreno Building in the Basak Campus. Senior High School students from different schools in Cebu and enthusiasts are invited to attend the workshop.

The letter is signed by Dr. Gregg Victor D. Gabison, Dean of CICCT.
