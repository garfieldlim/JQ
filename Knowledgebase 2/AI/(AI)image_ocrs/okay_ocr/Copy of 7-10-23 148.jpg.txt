B. USJ-R Shall:

1. Select, recommend and endorse students to WESERV for deployment under the CORPORATE INTERNSHIP PROGRAM pursuant
to the following conditions:

a. For Information Technology and Information Systems students; minimum of 500 training hours.

b. For Computer Science students: minimum of 300 training hours.

2. Provide WESERV with the list of selected and endorsed student-trainees with their respective personal information, course, year level and other pertinent data for easy reference; |

3. Designate from among its faculty members an Program Director who will be assigned the following responsibilities:

a. Serve as liaison officer between WESERV and USJ-R;
b. Monitor student performance, accomplishments and progress during their internship program;
c. Conduct semestrial evaluation conference with the industry supervisor to discuss any problems that may
arise and for the purpose of evaluating the effectiveness of the Internship Program;
d. Provide WESERV with a written report on the evaluation and performance of student-trainees after every
internship program. |

4. Assign an academic adviser who will be responsible for the academic progress of the students during their internship period.

C. In the event that any of the student-trainee fails to complete the required training hours or if his performance is below
standard, USJ-R shall immediately replace such trainee with a qualified student and notify WESERV thereof.

D. The parties agree that this MOA embodies the entire agreement between them and supersedes all prior discussions, understandings and agreements whether written or verbal, including any previous Memoranda of Agreement entered into by the parties on the same subject matter. |

E. This MOA shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Memorandum of Agreement on the day and year first above written.
