University of San Jose-Recoletos

Commonwealth Avenue, P. del Rosario Street, 6000 Cebu City, Philippines

OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

TO: All Concerned
DATE: March 25, 2020
THROUGH: The Deans, Directors, Principals, and Office Heads

SUBJECT: Work Suspension up to April 14, 2020

In response to the crisis brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic, work shall be suspended from March 26, 2020 up to April 14, 2020 in both campuses (Main and Basak). For offices with critical functions, the office heads concerned shall be in-charge of giving instructions to their respective personnel. Admission tests are likewise cancelled until further notice.

Everyone is highly urged to stay at home and observe the strict precautionary measures during the days with no office and work transactions.

Let us be one Josenian family in these trying times. God bless us all!
