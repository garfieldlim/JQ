B. USJ-R Shall:

1. Recommend to TAI-PAN DEVELOPMENT INCORPORATED student participants who will be given the opportunity under this agreement;

2. Provide TAI-PAN DEVELOPMENT INCORPORATED with the necessary information concerning each recommended trainee's academic performance, skills and abilities to enable the Industry Supervisor to better evaluate their training needs;

3. Assign a faculty adviser who will be responsible for monitoring the progress of student trainees and coordinating with TAI-PAN DEVELOPMENT INCORPORATED regarding the same;

4. Require the student-trainees to sign a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) setting forth the terms and conditions of their training program;

5. Require each student trainee to undergo an orientation seminar before the commencement of the training program;

6. Evaluate the performance of the student-trainees and submit the evaluation report to TAI-PAN DEVELOPMENT INCORPORATED at the end of their training period;

7. Issue a Certificate of Completion of Training (COCT) to the student-trainees who successfully completed the program.

D. This Agreement is executable by signing by the parties hereto.
