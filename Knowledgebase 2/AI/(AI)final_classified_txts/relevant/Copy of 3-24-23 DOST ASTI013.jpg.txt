1. This MOA shall remain in force and effect for a period of three (3) years from the Effective Date, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this MOA.
2. Either party may terminate this MOA upon written notice to the other at least thirty (30) days before the end of any yearly period during the term hereof.
3. Termination of this MOA shall not affect the rights and liabilities of either party that have accrued prior to the date of termination.
