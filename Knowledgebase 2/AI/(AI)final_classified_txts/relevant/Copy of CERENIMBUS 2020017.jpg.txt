
B. USJ-R Shall:

1. Endorse to CERENIMBUS only students who are currently enrolled in good standing in the university,
with appropriate academic qualifications and clearance from their respective departments;

2. Provide a Certificate of Enrollment to each student before they commence training at CERENIMBUS,
which will serve as proof of their status as full-time students of USJ-R during the duration of the training program;

3. Issue to the trainee-students an official transcript of record detailing the number of hours that they have
accomplished under the CORPORATE INTERSHIP PROGRAM in CERENIMBUS, which shall be signed by the respective
department chairperson and the University President;

4. Require each trainee-student to sign a Training Agreement with CERENIMBUS acknowledging that they are
aware of the program's requirements and that they agree to abide by the rules and regulations set forth by both
institutions; and

5. Indemnify, hold harmless and defend CERENIMBUS from any claims arising from any injury or damage sustained
by the student-trainees during the course of their training in the company.

C. General Provisions:

1. This Memorandum of Agreement shall take effect upon signing by both parties and shall remain in full force
and effect for a period of one (1) year, unless sooner revoked or terminated by either party for any valid reason;

2. No modification of this Memorandum of Agreement shall be effective unless consented to in writing by both
parties;
