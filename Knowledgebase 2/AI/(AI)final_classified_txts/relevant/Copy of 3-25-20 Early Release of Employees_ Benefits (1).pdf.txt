
OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Memorandum No. 2020-031
Subject: Early Release of Sick Leave Conversion and Medicine Allowance
Date: March 24, 2020

In view of the serious threat posed by COVID-19 and its adverse impact on our employees, the University Administration is extending its financial support through an early release of the monetized sick leave credits and medicine allowance on March 31, 2020.

Attached to this memorandum are the detailed guidelines for the implementation of the early release of these benefits.

Let us all stay safe everyone!
