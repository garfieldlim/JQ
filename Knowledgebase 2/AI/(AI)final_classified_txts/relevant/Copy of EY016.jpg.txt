
The confidentiality obligation shall remain in effect for a period of five years after the termination or expiration of this MOU. Confidential information does not include any information that is: (i) already known to Educational Institute at the time of disclosure by EY GDS without confidentiality obligations, (ii) rightfully received from a third party who did not have an obligation to keep such information confidential, or (iii) becomes publicly available through no fault or breach of Educational Institute.

Educational Institute shall take all reasonable precautions and measures to ensure the security and protection of the Confidential Information of EY GDS from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. This includes ensuring that any staff members with access to the Confidential Information are bound by confidentiality obligations.

In the event that Educational Institute is required by law or court order to disclose the Confidential Information, it shall provide prompt notice to EY GDS and cooperate in any effort to prevent such disclosure.
