CAMPUS MINISTRY OFFICE

April 2, 2019

TO: OFFICE HEADS, DEANS, FACULTY & STAFF
RE: ADLAW SA PAGPAMALANDONG (Holy Week Reflections)

You are all invited to take part of our annual Adlaw sa Pagpamalandong (Holy Week Reflections) with Rev. Fr. Rogie M. Quinga, RCJ on Holy Wednesday, April 17, 2019 at the Center for Performing Arts. Below is the schedule of activities:

7:30 - 8:30 a.m.: Registration

8:30 - 8:35 a.m.: Morning Prayer

8:35 - 8:40 a.m.: Ice Breaker

8:40 - 8:45 a.m.: Introduction of Speaker

8:45 - 9:45 a.m.: Talk: Fr. Rogie M. Quinga, RCJ

9:45 - 10:00 a.m.: Open Forum

10:00 - 10:15 a.m.: Snacks

10:15 - 11:15 a.m.: Exposition of the Blessed Sacrament/ Confessions
11:15 a.m.: Holy Mass

To facilitate our preparation, please confirm with the Campus Ministry Office the number of faculty/staff/personnel from your office who are coming to the abovementioned activity not later than Friday, April 12, 2019. Thank you for your cooperation.

May we be one in our journey with our Lord in His Paschal Mystery.

Rev. Fr. Arian Josep Campus Ministry Director VP for Religious Affairs
