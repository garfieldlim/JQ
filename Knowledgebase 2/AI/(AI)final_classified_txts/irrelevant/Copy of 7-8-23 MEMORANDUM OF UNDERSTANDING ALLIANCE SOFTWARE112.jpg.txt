Article 1 - DEFINITIONS
As used in this Memorandum of Understanding, unless otherwise indicated by the context, the following terms shall have the meanings assigned to them:

a. “Program” refers to an industry-academe linkage program as defined in the Preamble;
b. “Parties” shall refer to ALLIANCE and USJ-R collectively;
c. “Faculty” shall refer to the teaching staff of the University.
d. “Student” shall refer to any person enrolled at the University for purposes of education.
e. “Employee” refers to any individual who performs work or services under an employment contract with ALLIANCE, including but not limited to regular and temporary employees and consultants;
f. “Technology” shall mean the tools, systems, techniques, and processes that are used by ALLIANCE in its business operations, which may include software, hardware, and methodologies;
g. “Confidential Information” refers to information that is proprietary or confidential and is provided by one party to the other party under this Memorandum of Understanding, whether such information is oral, written, or in electronic form; and
