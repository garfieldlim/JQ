Tandang Sora Hall, Osmeña Blvd., Cebu City

National Training Workshop on Research Instrumentation (NTWRI) 2020

The Commission on Higher Education Regional Office VII endorses the above undertaking with the following objectives:
1. To understand the concepts in Classical Test Theory and Item Response Theory in a Research Instrumentation;
2. To utilize classical test approaches in test development and validation; and,
3. To use Rasch-model in test development.

The activity will be conducted on February 22-23, 2020 at the Tandang Sora Hall, Cebu Normal University (CNU), Osmeña Blvd., Cebu City.

Registration is free on a first-come, first-served basis due to limited seats. Other expenses incurred during the activity will be shouldered by sending higher education institutions (HEIs).

Higher Education Institutions in Region VII are requested to support and participate in the said undertaking.
