OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

TO: All Concerned
March 16, 2020
THROUGH: The Deans, Directors, Principals, and Office Heads
SUBJECT: UPDATES

In consonance with the recent Executive Order No. 052 of Hon. Mayor Edgardo C. Labella, please be informed of the following updates:

1. Classes in all levels are suspended from March 16 to April 14, 2020.

2. There will be no conduct of final examinations from hereon even after the class suspension is lifted. Students will be evaluated on the basis of available indicators.

3. All other curricular activities for the remainder of the semester are cancelled. Please be guided accordingly.

Thank you and always keep safe.

REV. FR. RISTONER MASPARA, OAR
President
