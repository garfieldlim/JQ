The Commission on Higher Education Regional Office VII, hereby endorses
the above undertaking on “National Training Workshop on Research
Instrumentation (NTWRI) 2020” with the objectives for the participants to a)
Understand the concepts in Classical Test Theory and Item Response Theory in a
Research Instrumentation; b) Utilize classical test approaches in test development
and validation, and c) Use rasch-model in test development. The conduct of the
activity is on February 22-23, 2020 at the Tandang Sora Hall, Cebu Normal
University (CNU), Osmefia Blvd., Cebu City (please see attached copy for details).

Registration is FREE on a first-come-first-serve basis due to limited seats. —
Other expenses incurred during the said activity to be shouldered by the sending
higher education institutions (HEIs).
