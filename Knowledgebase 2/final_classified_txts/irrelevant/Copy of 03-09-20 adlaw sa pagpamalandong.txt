8:40 - 8:45 a.m. - Introduction of Speaker

8:45 — 9:45 a.m. - Talk: Fr. Joriz F. Calza, SDB

9:45— 10:10 a.m. - Snacks

10:10-11:10am. - Exposition of the Blessed Sacrament/ Confessions
11:10 - Holy Mass

To facilitate in our preparation, please confirm with the Campus Ministry Office the
