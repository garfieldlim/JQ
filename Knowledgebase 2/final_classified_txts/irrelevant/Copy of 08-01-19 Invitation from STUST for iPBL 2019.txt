INVITATION

Dear Chairman Cuizon,

On behalf of STUST, I am pleased to extend our sincere welcome to your colleagues and students to
participate in this year’s international Project Based Learning program at Southern Taiwan
University of Science and Technology (STUST). The program that STUST and Osaka Institute of
Technology (OIT), Japan started in 2017 is focused on Robotics wherein we let students from both
universities to work together and accomplish a goal. This year marks the third year of the program
and we are excited to have faculty and students from the University of San Jose Recoletos (USJR)
to join as the third university and third country for this program. The 2019 i-PBL between STUST,
OIT and USJR would be very exciting as it brings in students from various fields and nationalities
to come together and solve a common challenge. We would like to invite the USJR delegation from
the 8 of August 2019 to the 17 of August 2019. Your tentative itinerary is shown in Appendix 1.
Students will be housed in the university dormitory together with the Japanese and Taiwanese
students in a 4-person room. The 2019 i-PBL program is subsidized by the Ministry of Education
Office of Higher Education Sprout Project and STUST. The 2019 i-PBL will cover for the
dormitory, project materials and airport transfer from Kaohsiung International Airport to STUST.
