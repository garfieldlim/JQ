



OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT





TO		:	ALL CONCERNED					August 7, 2020


SUBJECT	:	NOMENCLATURE OF THE DIFFERENT COLLEGES



As approved by the School Board, we would like to inform you that the nomenclature of the different colleges shall be changed/updated effective AY 2020-2021; to wit:

From College of Arts & Sciences to School of Arts and Sciences

From College of Education to School of Education

From College of Engineering to School of Engineering

From College of Commerce to School of Business & Management

From College of Information, Communication & Computer technology to School of Computer Studies

From College of Nursing to School of Allied Medical Sciences

Please be guided accordingly.

Thank you.




							
REV. FR. CRISTOPHER C. MASPARA, OAR
President
